The Little Girl in the Dark Forest

Black Forest Group presents the play of the awarded French writer Philippe Minyana ‘The Little Girl in the Dark Forest‘, an adaptation of the myth ‘Procne and Philomela’ deriving from ‘Metamorphoses’ by Ovid.A multimedia play; a synthesis of theatre, cinematography, microsculpture, video-art and music under the direction of Pantelis Dentakis. "The Little Girl in the Dark Forest" is presented in Athens in KET, from January 20th to April 14, 2020th, supported by the French Institute of Greece.
